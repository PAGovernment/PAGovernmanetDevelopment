For officers in the line of duty, nothing is more important than choosing the right equipment.

If your suppliers only show up for invoices, you deserve something better. Primary Arms Law Enforcement is a full-service Law Enforcement partner, dedicated to enhancing your procurement experience. From first trial to final support and advising, PALE works as a companion for superior service.

Procurement Support

We're with you every step of the way. Our experienced team can match you with the right product, instantly providing our lowest price available. Any quote is answered on a 24-hour schedule, ensuring that your needs are met quickly.

Testing/Evaluation

Testing and Evaluation is key to any department-level purchase decision, so we support our partners with trial equipment. Our T&E program opens access to hundreds of brands, including our exclusive Primary Arms Optics, home of the highly effective ACSS reticle.

Cadet Rifle Catalog

Your cadets are at their best when they get firsthand experience with their new equipment. Our Cadet Rifle Catalog allows cadets in training to purchase deeply-discounted equipment from our curated list of duty-grade rifles and accessories.

Product Demo Days

Familiarization with new products takes experience. To improve your team's exposure, we work with the industry's top brands to schedule department wide demo days, including exclusive access to Primary Arms Optics products.

Trade-In Program

Expand your budget by trading in retired equipment. We offer competitive pricing on all items, always offering our best price first. Partnering with GTI's Asset Trading Program, your old kit will support underfunded agencies around the US.

Individual Officer Program

On top of department purchases, your officers will receive a wide variety of individual discounts on the industry's top brands and products. No matter what kind of product they’re looking for, we’ll provide our best service and support for any purchase.
